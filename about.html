I am a certified scrum master I have also been responsible for a re-alignment of development practices at the NSW RFS, bringing in agile methodologies (Scrum) and a number of Development Processes such as git(hub) and CI.

I enjoy a challenge. I enjoy Open Source. I believe there is a better way. I strongly believe Open Source holds the key to the future sustainability of the IT world. Check out my Github account for more information on what I'm into.
