THE THINKING

I am currently employed as a ‘Senior Enterprise Architect’ at the NSW Rural Fire Service where I am responsible for planning and implementing enterprise architecture to ensure resilient, modern and reliable systems for the management of rural fires and emergency incidents in NSW (and ACT).

I am a certified scrum master I have also been responsible for a re-alignment of development practices at the NSW RFS, bringing in agile methodologies (Scrum) and a number of Development Processes such as git(hub) and CI.

Prior to my current position I was the ‘Mobility Solutions Manager’ at a Sydney start-up, charged with leading a team of Consultants in SAP Integration projects ranging from ‘simple’ web applications to even ‘simpler’ (thanks to Xamarin Mobile) native mobile apps… and everything in between.

In my personal life I enjoy going to the gym and I volunteer with a Rural Fire Brigade in Sydney's urban-bushland interface, where I hold a position as an Officer of the brigade. I am also a member of the NSW RFS Remote Area Firefighting team, qualified in helicopter insertion and remote area specialist operations.

I enjoy a challenge. I enjoy Open Source. I believe there is a better way. I strongly believe Open Source holds the key to the future sustainability of the IT world.
